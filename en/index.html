Who we are

We don't create games — we create experiences

Let’s Party — is a project born from friendship, energy, and a love for vibrant celebrations.

We are Anastasia and Diana, two friends who have been at the heart of events since childhood: drama club, school holidays, scripts, and parties for friends — simply because we were genuinely interested in it.

Back in eighth grade, after staging a play, we came up with the name Dynasty, combining our names and our dream of having our own business. In 2025, this dream took the form of an event production company — Dynasty Events. But we decided to start with the Let’s Party project.

Anastasia — co-founder and host since 2022, with over 500 events under her belt. For her, it's not about "delivering a format" but about creating an atmosphere that suits each specific group.

Diana — co-founder with entrepreneurial experience since 2020. she easily finds a connection with anyone, knows how to spark interest, negotiate, and create a comfortable, lively atmosphere.

We are identical in vibe, energy, and attitude toward our work — and that's exactly what makes our team whole.

Party Mafia — our headliner +

Party Mafia is a unique format, a fresh take on the familiar "Mafia". Not just a game, but a full program for your holiday. Participants are given the opportunity to show their acting skills, reveal themselves from a new side, and snatch victory through their knowledge, agility, reaction, and wit. It's a format with a lot of emotion and dynamics, where guests loosen up and get involved in the process. Suitable for groups that want drive, movement, and vivid memories.

Bunker: Immersive Experience +

This format is for those who want maximum immersion in the atmosphere of apocalypse and survival. We take the classic "Bunker" as a basis and create a unique gaming experience for your group: with a well-thought-out concept, atmosphere, and details that unfold gradually. Guests know their profession and backstory in advance, and then the game unfolds into a whole story at the location itself. This is a format for those who want to not just play, but live the game!

Classic "Mafia" +

A strategic, psychological, and intellectual game for calm groups. The perfect format to unobtrusively complement an evening for 1–2 hours: socialize, observe, engage logic, and feel the excitement without extra noise. Suitable for those who love classics, atmosphere, and a steady pace.

Bunker — classic format +

A survival game where the winner is not the loudest, but the most persuasive. Each player receives a unique character trait, and their main task is to prove why they deserve to enter the bunker and how they can help humanity cope with the impending catastrophe. There's no need to lie here — it's important to argue, think strategically, and read people. The game captivates, unites, and often reveals participants from a completely new side.

Producer's Approach

We don't work with templates. Each format is adapted to the group, mood, and goal of the event! We ask guiding questions and love it when you tell us about your company so we can come up with unique features just for you.

Lively and Gentle Engagement

We know how to create dynamics without pressure! We definitely won't force anyone. Active guests get space to play, while calmer ones feel confident and naturally join the process.

Sense of the Room and Atmosphere

We carefully read the group's mood and manage the pace of the evening so it doesn't "sag" or get tiring. The game develops organically, and the atmosphere remains lively and warm.

Multilingual Format

We host events in Russian, Latvian, and English, including bilingual formats — so that every guest feels equal and doesn't drop out of the process.

Flexibility and Comfort

We adapt the format to the people, not the people to the format. We consider timing, the venue, and the guest list to ensure the evening is convenient and logical for all participants.
