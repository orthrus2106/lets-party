Party Mafia ‚Äî our headliner +

Party Mafia is a unique format, a fresh take on the familiar "Mafia". Not just a game, but a full program for your holiday. Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How long does the game last? +

It depends on the number of participants and their mood. We always ask clients if there was previous experience with such games and what the expectations are for the evening. Hosts try to adjust to the timing if one was specified.

How many guests can play Mafia? +

From 6 to 25. We discuss this point in advance and agree on the game dynamics.

What languages are the events held in? +

In Latvian, English, and Russian. Bilingual hosting is also possible.

How many players can play Bunker? +

From 5 to 20. We discuss this point in advance and agree on the game dynamics.

Are travel expenses included in the price? +

No, travel expenses are paid separately depending on the location. Within Riga, we only charge for parking.

Do you host games for children? +

Yes, starting from 8 years old. But if you need programs for younger children, we have partners and can help you organize a children's party as well.
