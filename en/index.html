Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How long does the game last? +

It depends on the number of participants and their mood. We always clarify with clients whether they have prior experience and what their expectations are. Hosts try to adapt to the timing if one has been specified.

What is the guest count for a Mafia game? +

From 6 to 25 players. We discuss this in advance to ensure the best game dynamics.

In what languages are the events conducted? +

In Latvian, English, and Russian. Bilingual events are also possible.

How many players can play Bunker? +

From 5 to 20 players. We discuss the details and dynamics in advance.

Are travel expenses included in the price? +

No, travel expenses are paid separately depending on the location. In Riga, we only charge for parking.

Do you run games for children? +

Yes, starting from 8 years old. If you need programs for younger children, we have partners and can help you organize a kids' party.
